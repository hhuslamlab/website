Prof. Kevin Tang participated as a co-designer and speaker in the upcoming Young Academy of Europe (YAE) event: 

“Language and Speech Biomarkers of Acquired and Developmental Disorders: Co-designing an Interdisciplinary Approach”

📅 11 March 2026 | 🕛 12:00–14:00 CET

Event link: [https://yacadeuro.org/language-and-speech-biomarkers-of-acquired-and-developmental-disorders-co-designing-an-interdisciplinary-approach-11-march-2026-12-14-cet/](https://yacadeuro.org/language-and-speech-biomarkers-of-acquired-and-developmental-disorders-co-designing-an-interdisciplinary-approach-11-march-2026-12-14-cet/)

Organised by the Young Academy of Europe (YAE), this interactive event brings together researchers to collaboratively design an interdisciplinary research project in real time. The session aims to:

* Explore how interdisciplinary research projects are developed in theory and practice
* Share experiences and strategies for successful interdisciplinary collaboration
* Build capacity across disciplines within the European research community

Prof. Tang joined colleagues Ioana Podina, Valantis Fyndanis, and Georgios Georgiou in leading the live co-design session, contributing expertise on language and speech biomarkers in acquired and developmental disorders.

This event is open to YAE members, but also any others interested in discussing the dynamics of interdisciplinary research co-design.
